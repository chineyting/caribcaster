When the miniCASTER® encoder hit the market, it was rapidly awarded as “best of broadcast design” by Broadcast engineering. An HD encoder, so small and portable including a battery onboard was a big surprise for the market. Meanwhile a lot of successors are desperately trying to beat miniCASTER®.

miniCASTER® set the standard for ultra-mobile high quality encoding. It is a professional producers‘ choice, when it comes to reliabilty and versatility. But you, the customers wanted more. So we made the award-winning miniCASTER® even better and called it the Loop. The new Loop is ultra mobile, battery packed and as powerful as its predecessor, but with more features for professional users. It takes SDI signals, of course, but now with interlaced encoding for broadcast signals (576i, 1080i) and a new, second SDI (out) connector for looping through the signal to a monitor, a recorder, a mixer. Giving you full control over your picture.

We added a new easy selection of broadcast output-modes such as SD-PAL/NTSC in 4:3/16:9, 720p50/p60, 1080i50/i60, allowing you to create your own custom-output-modes. With predefined output modes (video standards) the encoding profiles are now available. A new close to constant-bit-rate feauture smoothens and protects the encoding signal from peaking while on UDP/TS, RTP or RTMP. The new Loop – better than ever.

The miniCASTER® SDI receiver is the perfect companion for decoding live signals. Of course we recommend the miniCASTER® encoder as the signal sending device, but it will also understand signals from third party decoders. Equipped with two SDI out connectors you can forward two SDI signal simultaneously.

In addition you can deliver six simultaneous streams to the decoder. The multiview function monitors all six signals by showing them on a screen. With its relay option the SDI receiver can forward the signal to other SDI receivers via IP with an unlimited distance. So one crew sends a signal but many TV-studios receive it in real time.
